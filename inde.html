Research Ethics Guidelines

Introduction

This document outlines the ethical principles and guidelines to be followed in the conduct of our research project.

Ethical Principles

  • Respect for Participants' Rights and Dignity
  • Transparency and Informed Consent
  • Confidentiality and Privacy
  • Integrity in Research Design and Conduct
  • Accountability and Responsibility

Code of Conduct

All individuals involved in the research project must adhere to the following code of conduct:

  • Conduct research with honesty and integrity.
  • Respect the rights and well-being of research participants.
  • Obtain informed consent from participants before their involvement.
  • Maintain confidentiality and privacy of participant information.
  • Report any ethical concerns promptly.

Conclusion

By following these ethical guidelines, we aim to ensure the credibility and integrity of our research project.
