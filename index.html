Additional Ethical Considerations

Informed Consent: Make sure all participants are aware of the research project being conducted and fully consent to participate.

Bias and Fairness: Be conscious of the potential bias in the research project's design and information collected for the project. Aim to take a balanced approach when considering opinions and information to ensure fairness.

Privacy and Confidentiality: Protect information collected from the research project by anonymizing and aggregating data that reveals personal or sensitive information. Communicate to participants how the data will be handled and stored.

Data Security: Data collected from the research will need to be secured and not be accessible to the public.

Integrity: Avoid financial or personal obligations to external entities in all aspects of the research project.
